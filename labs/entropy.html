Lab 03 – Entropy Sink

A toy for watching rebellion energy leak away.

This lab tracks a single scalar “energy” value over time. At each step, a fraction leaks into an unseen sink, while random agitation adds noise. You can adjust the sink strength and agitation amplitude. The graph shows how quickly structured activity decays under different leak regimes.

Reading it: the curve shows energy vs. time. High leak with low agitation quickly flattens the line at zero—analogous to revolt energy being continuously drained into Deck 0. Low leak with strong agitation produces persistent fluctuations.
