Beat Sheets (High-Level)

Teasers, act turns, and climaxes for each episode.

Episode 1 – The Rebellion Deck

  • Teaser: Storm at sea; donkeys struggle with flooding and broken beams.
  • Act I: Donkeys organize grievances; foxes promise hearings; Lorenzo mediates.
  • Act II: March toward upper decks fails as staircases warp and tilt.
  • Act III: Rebellion collapses; Lorenzo feels complicit; Bruno dreams of a warning Llama.

Episode 2 – Llama: Middle Manager of Heaven

  • Teaser: Lorenzo receives a promotion scroll.
  • Act I: New office, impossible job description, conflicting orders.
  • Act II: Filing system misclassifies donkey complaints; rules tighten.
  • Act III: Donkeys protest Lorenzo; dream-llama tells him he cannot be both bridge and gate.

Episode 3 – Ark of Infinite Worlds

  • Teaser: Bruno falls asleep amid scraps of the Donkey Gospel.
  • Act I: Wakes in cosmic space among countless Arks.
  • Act II: Visits or observes alternate geometries that fail in new ways.
  • Act III: Forced back into his Ark; realizes it is one option among many.

Episode 4 – The Donkey Gospel

  • Teaser: Donkeys whispering parables under dim lanterns.
  • Act I: Writing sessions; Bruno teaches memory tricks.
  • Act II: Foxes confiscate texts; lions denounce them.
  • Act III: Scrolls burned; Lorenzo and Bruno become living backups.

Episode 5 – Foxes at the Helm

  • Teaser: Foxes quietly mocking lion complacency.
  • Act I: Procedural coup; lions sign away authority.
  • Act II: Early reforms, minor improvements for donkeys.
  • Act III: Paranoia, surveillance, and authoritarianism return in fox form.

Episode 6 – The Lion’s Dream

  • Teaser: Aurus staring at his own reflection.
  • Act I: Confession of desire to descend; first failed attempt.
  • Act II: Fox panic and donkey confusion; Bruno explains role-prison.
  • Act III: Final attempt fails; lion accepts his confinement.

Episode 7 – Noah’s Revision

  • Teaser: Noah muttering over impossible angles on his blueprints.
  • Act I: Small fixes trigger minor structural strain.
  • Act II: Bruno encourages bolder reconfiguration; foxes panic.
  • Act III: Major attempt nearly breaks the Ark; Noah disappears.

Episode 8 – The Bureau of Arks

  • Teaser: Lorenzo wakes in a cosmic office full of files labeled with worlds.
  • Act I: Meets Archivists; files grievances.
  • Act II: Complaints loop, committees proliferate, bureaucracy glitched.
  • Act III: Bureau meltdown ejects Lorenzo back into the Ark.

Episode 9 – Deck 0: The Unseen Layer

  • Teaser: Bruno falls through a gap in the floor.
  • Act I: Explores silent tension-draining beings.
  • Act II: Learns Deck 0 is a hidden entropy sink that stabilizes hierarchy.
  • Act III: Returns with this knowledge; Ark becomes audibly unstable.

Episode 10 – Llama Ascendant

  • Teaser: Lorenzo walks between decks without touching stairs.
  • Act I: Gains movement freedom; elevated by foxes, feared by lions.
  • Act II: Attempts to flatten curvature; structure buckles.
  • Act III: Transcends the Ark, leaving it to collapse without its mediator.

Episode 11 – The Donkey Who Saw the Horizon

  • Teaser: Anice glimpses a bright line at the edge of the world.
  • Act I: Donkeys debate the vision; foxes dismiss; lions condemn.
  • Act II: March toward the bow; Ark tips and reveals void.
  • Act III: Horizon dissolves into stars; decision to “sail upward.”

Episode 12 – Bruno’s Trial, Performed by Animals

  • Teaser: Animals rehearsing a stylized trial-play.
  • Act I: Bruno enters as a scripted defendant.
  • Act II: Refuses lines; script and geometry break down.
  • Act III: Ark ceiling tears open; world dissolves into infinite Arks.
