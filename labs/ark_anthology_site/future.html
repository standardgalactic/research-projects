Future Seasons & Extensions

Where the Ark Anthology could drift next.

Season 2 — The Open Worlds

After the Ark dissolves into starlight, the camera (and Bruno’s perspective) drifts to the other Arks glimpsed in Episode 3. Each episode follows a different Ark with a distinct geometry: egalitarian failures, spiral-world rebellions, post-hierarchical experiments that threaten to collapse. The core question: if hierarchy is a geometry problem, what does a stable non-hierarchical manifold look like?

Season 3 — The Llama Returns

Lorenzo reappears as a fully-fledged field-avatar, no longer bound to a single Ark. He moves across worlds as a kind of cosmic mediator, helping (or failing to help) new civilizations design their own deck structures. Episodes track his attempts to avoid repeating the capture he experienced in Season 1.

Season 4 — Trial at the Center of the Manifold

Bruno is summoned (or dream-summoned) to a tribunal at the center of the Ark manifold, where representatives of many worlds debate whether multiplicity itself is heresy. The season blends cosmic courtroom drama with RSVP field theory: Bruno must argue that no single Ark, no single geometry, can be final.

Other Possible Extensions

  • Ark Anthology: Stories from Deck 0 – a darker anthology diving deeper into the unseen layer.
  • Bruno & The Bureau – a bureaucratic spin-off focused on the cosmic office where Arks are designed.
  • The Donkey Gospel (Standalone Film) – the rise and suppression of the lower-deck scripture, framed as a tragic musical.
