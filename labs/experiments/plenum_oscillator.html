Lab 07 – Plenum Oscillator

A damped driven oscillator as the plenum’s breathing mode.

The scalar mode \(\Phi(t)\) obeys \[ \ddot{\Phi} + \eta \dot{\Phi} + k \Phi = \xi(t), \] with \(k\) the restoring force, \(\eta\) the damping, and \(\xi\) a noise term. This lab shows the time-series of \(\Phi\) as a green trace.

Reading it: lightly damped oscillators with moderate noise wander through sustained quasi-cycles—RSVP’s picture of a plenum always slightly out of equilibrium. Heavy damping collapses motion; high noise shreds coherent cycles into jitter.
