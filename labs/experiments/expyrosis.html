Lab 10 – Expyrosis Reset

Everything freezes; one fluctuation restarts structure.

A cloud of points slows under increasing “freeze rate.” When the global kinetic energy falls below a threshold, the system is reset by seeding a small instability: a single point is kicked, and motion reappears.

Reading it: dots represent local structure; their motion is the plenum’s temperature. As freeze rate rises, velocities vanish and the picture stops. When kinetic energy crosses the threshold, a seed kicks the system back into motion—RSVP’s expyrotic universe that smooths, then re-ignites.
