Lab 09 – Semantic Horizon

A radial field smoothing outward until the signal fades.

A 1D radial profile \(\Phi(r)\) evolves under diffusion and damping: \[ \partial_t \Phi = \sigma \nabla^2 \Phi - \alpha \Phi. \] A pulse is emitted at the center; you can tune smoothing \(\sigma\) and damping \(\alpha\).

Reading it: brightness around the ring encodes \(\Phi(r)\). With high smoothing and low damping, the pulse spreads far before fading—wide semantic reach. With low smoothing or high damping, signals die close to the source, defining a tight horizon.
