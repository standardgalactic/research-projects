Lab 06 – Deck 0 Reservoir

Visible deck vs. hidden Deck 0: watching energy leak and burp back.

Two coupled reservoirs: a visible deck and a hidden Deck 0. Energy leaks from visible to hidden at rate \(\gamma\), with occasional noisy backflow. You can toggle the leak and adjust amplitude.

Top bar: visible deck. Bottom bar: Deck 0. Line: visible energy over time.
Reading it: with strong leak and low backflow, the visible bar drains while Deck 0 grows, and the time-trace flattens at zero. With weaker leak or stronger backflow, the visible deck never fully settles—RSVP’s picture of a world where rebellion energy keeps trying to escape the sink.
