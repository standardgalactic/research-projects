Current gap

1.2 Coupling Constants Calibration
Current gap
: Constants {α, β, γ, λ, κ, ν, ξ} are defined symbolically but lack empirical grounding.
To extend

Derive dimensional analysis: [α] = [trust/interaction], [β] = [entropy/information], etc.
Propose measurement protocols linking each constant to observable social metrics
Estimate ranges from historical trust data (GSS, Gallup) via parameter fitting
Establish stability constraints: e.g., λν > γκ for oscillatory vs. overdamped regimes
